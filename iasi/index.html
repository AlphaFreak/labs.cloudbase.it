Cloudbase LABS @Iași

Cloudbase Solutions is organizing a Python & DevOps training in Iasi. In this effort we will mentor and assist a group of 10 students over a period of 2 weeks, covering the following topics: Python, Git, Linux / Windows and shell scripting (Bash / PowerShell).

We’re doing this because we like open source projects and we love Python. It’s a small community, but we want to see it grow. Whether it’s OpenStack or any other open source project, we hope that after 2 weeks you’ll start contributing to community driven projects and want to get involved in open source.

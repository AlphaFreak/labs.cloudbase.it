Cloudbase LABS @Timisoara

Cloudbase Solutions is partnering with the Computer Science student organization from the “Politehnica” University of Timisoara in delivering a full semester of Python, OpenStack and Hyper-V hands-on workshops. The goal of this workshop is to be able to deploy an OpenStack cloud for development purposes, understand the general concepts and architecture, be able to look under the hood and debug, test previously implemented features and be able to fix simple reported bugs and start implementing Python code.

Please visit labs.ligaac.ro and find out more about when the next round of workshops are scheduled.
